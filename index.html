A team of gifted individuals in master Sedat's class have participated in an exhaustingly long 3 weeks to design and build an app that translates the ancient Sumerian language. While the team was short on time, they were able to finish most of it. We are hoping to fill this site with future projects.

Sumerian Transator

In this project, our team has planned on creating an OBS-like app that translates an image taken from a camera of an app and translate it to english. The team was successful in making the app however, they didn't have enough time to develop to use the local files. The captured image was going to be sent to the Python translation flowpipe via a web service. The web service we decided on, FastAPI, was confusing when combined with Kotlin so we have switched to Firebase. Hopefully the flowpipe and the final firebase file will be pushed in the GitHub repository.
